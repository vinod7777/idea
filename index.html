About Us

What is the AICTE IDEA Lab?

The AICTE IDEA Lab at Aditya Institute of Technology and Management (AITAM), Tekkali is a multidisciplinary innovation space designed to inspire creativity, encourage problem-solving, and nurture future-ready skills.

IDEA stands for Idea Development, Evaluation & Application — an initiative by AICTE to strengthen STEM hands-on learning and build a culture of entrepreneurship.
