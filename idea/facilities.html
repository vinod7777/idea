Our Facilities

Explore the hardware, software, and collaborative spaces available for the complete innovation lifecycle.

Hardware & Equipment

Core Equipment

Top-facility tools to convert your ideas into physical prototypes.

3D Printing - Additive Manufacturing

Create complex geometries and prototypes using FDM/SLA printers. Ideal for rapid prototyping models, functional parts, and custom components.

Rapid Prototyping FDM Printing Custom Parts

Laser Cutting - CNC Precision Fabrication

Computer-controlled cutting and engraving on various materials. Precise engineering for architectural models, acrylic cutting, and detailed designs.

CO2 Laser Cutting Engraving Acrylic / Wood

IoT Development Kits

A complete ecosystem for Internet of Things development. Microcontrollers, sensors, and actuators available to build smart devices and embedded systems from scratch.

Arduino Raspberry Pi Sensors & Actuators

Robotics & Automation Platforms

Industrial and educational robotics kits to learn automation and AI. Resources for building autonomous robots, robotic arms, and intelligent vehicle systems.
Mobile Robots Robotic Arms Automation

Vinyl Cutting & Printing

Professional vinyl cutting and printing machines for branding, sticker production, T-shirt printing, and poster making. Perfect for club events and startup product branding.

Vinyl Cutter Large Format Print Branding

Electronics Workbenches

Fully equipped electronics benches with soldering stations, oscilloscopes, multimeters, and testing kits. The ideal station for circuit design and testing electronic hardware prototypes.

Soldering Stations Oscilloscopes PCB Design

Work Spaces

Collaborative Work Areas

Design Thinking Studio

A flexible, open-plan space for brainstorming sessions, ideation workshops, and collaborative thinking.

High-Performance Workstations

Ergonomic area equipped with powerful PCs for CAD modelling, simulations, and rendering tasks.

Presentation & Demo Area

Area specifically designed for pitching ideas, presenting prototypes, and hosting guest lectures or hackathons.

Fabrication Zone

Dedicated space for heavy machinery, power tools, and safety equipment for physical prototyping work.

Meeting Rooms

Private discussion rooms with glass walls for project planning, team huddles, and mentor interactions.

Safety First

All student work areas are equipped with fire safety gear, first aid kits, eye protection, and safety training manuals.
