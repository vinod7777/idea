About IDEA Lab

Understand the purpose, vision, and mission behind AITAM's innovation hub.

What is IDEA Lab?

A Space Where Ideas Come Alive

At AITAM, we believe great ideas don't just happen in classrooms — they need a space to be explored, tested, and brought to life.

The AICTE IDEA Lab (Idea Development, Evaluation & Application Lab) at AITAM is a creative, hands-on innovation space where students, faculty, and community members collaborate to transform imagination into reality. It serves as a collaborative hub for engineering, science, management, and arts departments.

The Initiative

National AICTE IDEA Lab Programme

AICTE's IDEA Lab is a national initiative established to encourage students and faculty in the application of STEM fundamentals towards enhanced hands-on experience.

21st Century Skills

Equipping students with critical thinking, problem solving, design thinking, collaboration, and lifelong learning skills.

Hands-On Learning

Moving beyond theory — IDEA Lab empowers students to "engage, explore, experience, express and excel" through real project work.

Multidisciplinary Hub

A common facility that promotes interdisciplinary collaboration across engineering, science, management, and arts departments.

Innovation Ecosystem

Creating a vibrant ecosystem where creativity flourishes and students develop into future innovators and entrepreneurs.

Industry Connect

Bridging academia and industry through collaborative projects, expert talks, and MoUs with leading companies.

Global Readiness

Preparing globally competent graduates through international exposure, advanced tools, and future-focused programs.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

  • To foster a culture of innovation and experiential learning among students and faculty
  • To develop a dynamic ecosystem for knowledge exchange between academia, industry, and society
  • To create a vibrant culture of innovation and advanced research that impacts real-world challenges
  • To equip students with entrepreneurial skills and the confidence to build impactful ventures

Our Mission

  • Promote Innovation — create hands-on opportunities that go beyond textbooks
  • Foster Collaboration — build bridges between students, faculty, and industry
  • Encourage Excellence — blend engineering, science, management, and arts thinking
  • Empower for Impact — prepare students to solve real-world problems at scale
Who Can Join

The Lab is Open For Everyone

Students

All UG and PG students of AITAM can use the facilities and participate.

Faculty

Researchers

Ph.D. scholars can use advanced equipment for funded projects.

Community

Industry partners and startups can collaborate on innovation challenges.
