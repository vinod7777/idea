How to Reach

Getting to AITAM

By Train

The nearest railway station is Naupada Junction (NWP) which is approx 5km away. Alternatively, Srikakulam Road (CHE) is 50km away. You can take a bus or cab from there to the college.

By Bus

APSRTC buses operate regularly from Srikakulam, Vizianagaram, and other nearby towns to Tekkali. The college campus is conveniently located right on the NH-16 highway.

By Car / Taxi

AITAM is located on the Kolkata-Chennai National Highway (NH-16). It is around 140km from Visakhapatnam and 50km from Srikakulam.
