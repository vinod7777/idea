The 17 Global Goals

How We Contribute

The IDEA LAB curriculum and projects are mapped to achieve these goals through:

Quality Education (SDG 4)

Workshops on futuristic tech and mentoring projects for students and community.

Industry & Innovation (SDG 9)

Prototyping new ideas, fostering an incubation ecosystem, and supporting startups.

Climate Action (SDG 13)

Projects focused on energy efficiency, waste management, and sustainable materials.

Partnerships (SDG 17)

Collaborating with industry, study abroad programs, and inter-college events.
