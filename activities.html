Core Activities

A comprehensive range of innovation programs designed to build skills, spark creativity, and connect students to real opportunities.

Skill Development Workshops

Hands-on technical sessions and workshops covering key technologies like 3D printing, IoT, robotics, PCB design, and software programming.

Practical Skills Hands-on STEM

Hackathons, Ideathons & Problemathons

High-energy competitions where students solve real-world problems and tackle industry challenges within tight timeframes to innovate and design solutions.

Innovation Competitions Problem Solving

Interdisciplinary Projects

Mini and major projects that bring together students from various engineering branches to work collaboratively on complex engineering challenges.

Team Collaboration Projects Engineering

Startup & Entrepreneurship Support

Promoting innovation and guiding students from idea to startup. Helping incubate ideas, offering mentorship, and connecting students with funding opportunities.
Incubation Mentorship Startup School

Community Engagement & Outreach

Training programs for schools and polytechnic colleges to spark curiosity in STEM. Also extends help to local communities through technological interventions.

Social Impact Skill Enhancement Outreach

Product Design & Value Added Manufacturing

Helping students design and realize products using cutting-edge tools like CNC, Laser Cutters, and 3D Printers. Training on product lifecycle from concept to market.

Product Dev Prototyping Manufacturing

Collaborative Research & Innovation Challenges

Linkages with research labs and industry partners for cutting-edge projects. Encouraging students to apply for patents and publish research work.

Research Industry Connect Patents

Awareness & Demonstration Sessions

Regular weekly sessions to demonstrate the capabilities of the lab facilities, inviting guest speakers, experts, and fostering a culture of continuous learning.

Seminars Live Demos Guest Talks
Milestones

Lab Highlights

Oct 2021

AICTE IDEA Lab Inaugurated at AITAM

The lab was officially inaugurated by eminent dignitaries, opening its doors to all departments. A workshop on "Idea to Product" marked the grand opening.

Jan 2022

National Festival Hackathon - Season 1

A 24-hour national level hackathon witnessed by 300+ participants. Theme: "Smart Villages". 15 prototypes developed and 3 winning teams awarded.

Aug 2022

Gathering Champions - Season 2

An internal idea championship to scout talent. The selected champions continued to work on their ideas leading to patents. Focused on interdisciplinary solutions for campus problems.

Dec 2022

Idea Lab Equipment Procurement Drive

Major enhancement of lab facilities: Added 5-axis CNC, Industrial Robotic Arms, and advanced bio-sensors, significantly scaling the prototyping capabilities of the lab.

Mar 2023

International Collaboration

A memorandum of understanding (MoU) signed with global partners to encourage collaborative projects. Students participated in a global exchange program virtually.
